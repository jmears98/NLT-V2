Carbine Courses

No Limit Tactical's Carbine course will enhance and strengthen you capability and skills when handling and operating your carbine.

At NLT we train for the unpredictable future.

NLT will discusses and demonstrate proper carbine handling and fundamentals which will impact you overall marksmanship. We teach combat proven methods to give you a understanding of your rifles capabilities as well as the students. We will cover multiple learning objectives; nomenclature of your rifle and its optics, operation of your weapon proper manipulation, marksmanship, reloading, barricades, transitions, acquiring multiple targets, shooting and moving, and target discrimination.
