No Limit Tactical

Veteran Owned & Operated

Fred Wetzstein

Owner/Instructor

I served in the United States Coast Guard, active and reserve for 12 years. While serving, I attended USCG Maritime Law Enforcement Academy and served as a boarding officer and instructor. In 1996, I left active duty and attended the State of Delaware Corrections Academy. In 1998, I applied and was selected to attend the Corrections Emergency Response Team (CERT) Academy.

In September 2001, I was re-activated in the USCG due to 9-11. In November of that same year, I was hired as a federal law enforcement officer for the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) and attended the academy in March 2002. I'm currently still employed by DHS and have attended the Federal Firearms Training Program.

My skill set meets and exceeds the NRA's Range Masters qualifications. I hold myself to a high standard and continue to train to better my skill set to provide the best learning environment for all participants.

Eric Watkins

Firearms Instructor

Eric Watkins has over 27 years of service as a police officer in the State of Delaware. He retired in 2020 from Delaware Capitol Police, where he held the position of detective with the special investigative unit and was a member of the firearms training unit. He completed the Delaware State Police Academy in 1994 and has obtained instructor certifications in the following:

NRA police pistol, shotgun, and patrol rifle instructor, Blackwater patrol rife instructor, NTOA active shooter instructor, ASP Baton instructor, OC spray instructor, Combatives instructor, Glock armorer, Remington armorer and Blackwater AR-15 rifle armorer

Eric also has training in executive protection and provided that service to all three branches of state government. As a Council on Police Training (COPT) instructor, he has 25 years of firearms instructor experience qualifying and teaching large and small agencies, and providing individual instruction.
